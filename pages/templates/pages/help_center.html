Student/Parent: FAQs

What you need to know about The School Portal

How To Make Payments On The Portal?
You have to make transfer to the school's designated account first, after this login to this portal and check the "Payments/Bills" Menu and select "Make Payments". Enter the neccessary information in the form and submit.
How Do I Check My Results?
All your results are listed under the "Results" Menu. You can select; "First Term", "Second Term" or "Third Term" as the case may be. You can view/print your CA scores and download your results in pdf format.
How Does The E-Learning Works?
The e-learning tools allows you to explore lessons related to your class. Select e-learning center under "E-Learning" and check your classes materials. You can make comments, ask questions and interact with your teacher on the e-learning platform.
How About CBT ?
The portal users a third party CBT tools and your school will send you links whenever your need to take assessment test, exams or quizes.
How Do I Update My Profile ?
You can update basic information on your student portal. Use the "My Profile" Menu on the left side menu bar. Edit appropriately

Teacher/Staff/Admin: FAQs

All You Need To Know

Teacher's Role
Teachers are categorized as academic staff. Teacher has priviledge to access their student results and update same. Teacher takes daily attendance and ensure all information about their students are correct.
Staff Role
Non-academic staff can login to the portal if given the priviledge to perform task. This category of staff has limited priviledge on the portal.
Admin Role
There are different Admin Roles as may be stipulated by the school. The admin officers assist in portal issues and can be contacted for help in regards to the portal issues.
